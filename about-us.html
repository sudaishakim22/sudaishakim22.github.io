Mission

FROM PARK TO PLANET, WE'RE HELPING ANIMALS ACROSS THE GLOBE

SEEING THEM AT BluejackAWquarium SUPPORTS SAVING THEM IN THE WILD. Did you know? Your visit helps BluejackAWquarium provide $10 MILLION in donations and in-kind services every year for animal rescue and conservation efforts! Your trip to BluejackAWquarium supports rescuing thousands of animals in need every year.

Our Company

OVER 30,000 ANIMAL RESCUES.


BluejackAWquarium LEGACY OF ANIMAL RESCUE SPANS MORE THAN 50 YEARS In that time, the BluejackAWquarium Rescue Team has come to the aid of over 30,000 ill, injured and orphaned animals in need. These animals were given a second chance at life, thanks to the hard work and dedication of the BluejackAWquarium Rescue and Animal Care teams. The Rescue Team is on call 24/7/365
